WHO WE ARE

Engineering the Future of Ducting.

Master Duct Air-Condition Industries L.L.C, a leading manufacturer of Fire & Life safety products, Noise-control solutions, and HVAC ductwork.We stand as a premier name in the Middle East market is established in a spacious 10,000+ square feet facility located in the Modern Emirates Industrial Area. Today, we are proud to be a one-stop destination for all HVAC needs in the UAE. Over the time,we’ve established a strong reputation by supplying a diverse range of HVAC products to numerous projects across the country, playing a key role in major developments for government,semi-government, and private sectors.

At Master Duct, we are driven by our commitment to our clients, aiming to provide top-notch service backed by a team of experienced engineers and technical experts.

Equipped with the latest technology and fully automated machinery, Our team consists of passionate and skilled professionals who work together to achieve our goals. We are also committed to promoting environmentally friendly products within the HVAC industry, contributing to the sustainable growth of the UAE.

Our Mission

To deliver precision-engineered HVAC products with unmatched reliability, timely delivery, and superior customer service, while continually advancing sustainable manufacturing practices.

Our Vision

To lead the HVAC manufacturing industry through innovation, uncompromising quality, and eco-conscious engineering solutions.
