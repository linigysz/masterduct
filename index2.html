ABOUT US

What We Are.

Master Duct is a leading ducting and HVAC accessories manufacturing facility in the Middle East and a proud member of the GYSZ Group. Renowned for precision engineering, large-scale production, and uncompromising quality, we serve contractors and developers across the region with reliable, cost-effective solutions.

Our Mission

To provide world-class HVAC ducting solutions through precision engineering, cutting-edge automation, and a commitment to regional excellence.

Our Vision

To be the cornerstone of Middle Eastern infrastructure, setting the global standard for sustainable and innovative air distribution systems.
